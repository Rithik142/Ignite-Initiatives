Parent Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need any coding experience? +
Absolutely not — this camp is designed specifically for beginners. We start from the very basics and move at a pace that's comfortable for students who have never written a line of code. First-timers are not just welcome, they're exactly who we built this for.
What does my child need to bring? +
Students should bring their own laptop (Windows or Mac both work fine), a charger, and a packed lunch or snack. We'll handle everything else — software setup, materials, and instruction are all included in the tuition.
What grades and ages can attend? +
The camp is open to students currently in grades 5 through 8. This typically covers ages 10–14. If you're unsure whether your child is a good fit, feel free to reach out at igniteinitiatives@gmail.com and we're happy to chat.
When and how is payment due? +
Payment of $125 is due after we confirm your registration — within 24 hours of receiving your form, we'll email you with payment instructions. We accept Cash, Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, and Cash App. Your spot is secured once payment is received.
What is the drop-off and pick-up process? +
Camp runs 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM each day. Drop-off begins at 9:45 AM. The camp is located at 2150 Association Dr, Suite 270, Okemos, MI 48864. More logistics details will be sent in your confirmation email after registration.
Is there a refund policy? +
We understand that plans change. Please reach out to us at igniteinitiatives@gmail.com as soon as possible if you need to cancel. We'll do our best to accommodate refund requests made before June 1, 2025.
How many students are in the camp? +
We cap enrollment at 35 students to ensure every child gets individual attention and support throughout the week. This small group size is intentional — it creates a welcoming atmosphere and allows for personalized instruction.
